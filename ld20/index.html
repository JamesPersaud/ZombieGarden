Fabulous gigantic jewels magically appear in the garden of wonders. Unfortunately, so do ZOMBIES

Navigate the garden maze with W,A,S,D or the ARROW KEYS
Use the SPACE bar to fire your gun, but beware, it takes a while to reload. And you won't be able to use it when carrying a heavy jewel.

Joining the game will assign you to either the RED TEAM or the BLUE TEAM - try to capture the most treasure for your team.

And remember, it's suicide to go alone - take backup!
